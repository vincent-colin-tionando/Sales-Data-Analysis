# Sales Performance Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project aims to analyze sales performance based on:
- Sales
- Product Category
- Region
- Customer Segment

The analysis is complemented by an interactive dashboard to support data-driven strategic decision-making.

## 📊 Business Problem
The retail company operates across multiple regions and customer segments with diverse product categories. However, management lacks clear visibility into:

- Regional sales performance
- Product category contribution to overall sales
- Customer segment purchasing behavior
- Sales trends over time

Without structured analysis and visualization, strategic decisions regarding marketing allocation, regional expansion, and product prioritization may not be optimal.

## 🎯 Objectives
- Evaluate overall sales performance.
- Identify top and underperforming product categories.
- Analyze sales distribution across regions and states.
- Examine sales contribution by customer segment.
- Generate actionable business insights and recommendations.
